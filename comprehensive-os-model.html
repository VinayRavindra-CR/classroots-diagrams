Interconnections — how the rings touch

Surfaces ↔ UKG — every learner action reads/writes the UKG (RAG state, mastery, misconceptions) at runtime
Content → UKG — authored modules + spines + keystones are tagged into UKG nodes at publish
Business OS → Surfaces — Sales/Marketing acquires into Surfaces; Money handles subscription/payment within Surfaces; Programme Ops runs CERDAS rollouts on Byju's TV
Business OS → UKG entities — Counsellor/Parent & Community read Learner + Session entities for outreach
Platform integration → Business OS — Payment rails feed Money; SSO feeds Identity; SIS/LMS feeds Institutional GTM
Platform pillars ⤳ everything — Identity, Security, Audit, Governance apply across all three rings
